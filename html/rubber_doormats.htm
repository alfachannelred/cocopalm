RUBBER Door MATS

Kerala is the largest producer of India’s natural rubber. We produce our natural rubber mats from this natural resource. Rubber mats are best used as out door mats in different forms and sizes. They are highly durable and elegant.

RING MAT
This is a multipurpose heavy-duty mat can be used both indoors and outdoors as entrance mat, bathmat, laundry mat, snow mat, scraper mat or garden mat under all weather conditions. It is durable, washable and reversible. The open hole design on top with channels act as wipers and helps maximize dirt trappings. And the studded bottom adds to comfort while enhancing drainage and cleanliness .Ring mats are of modular design and can be joined to cover large floor area by using joiners.

STUD MAT
Stud mats have hundreds of flexible rubber studs and bristles acting as a buoyant cushion. The flexible rubber studs scrapes off the dirt and grit on your shoe sole and deposit them on the mat base where the beveled safety edges form a retaining wall that keeps dirt captive. These mats have an acupressure effect and helps in minimizing fatigue for jobs that involve prolonged standing.
