Jute Rugs

JUTE PRODUCTS
Jute is one of the finest and softest of natural floor covering materials. It is made from the yarn derived from the fibrous stalks of the jute plant and woven into either a boucle or herringbone pattern to make either rugs or matting runners. Jute fibre can be bleached to produce shades of pale cream to pale white.

Jute Rugs are very elegant and durable and they are a great way to add a touch of class and comfort to any room. Jute rugs, known for its natural color and appearance, can be dyed and colored to add variety to the wide range of rugs. The collection of jute rugs are available in all colors, shapes and sizes. Jute rugs are 100% pure natural jute .They can be made non-skid with latex backing.They are available in different sizes with 100% cotton binding, fringed ends or tucked ends.
